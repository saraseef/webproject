Vacationing in Oahu Hawaii

While vacationing in Oahu, Hawaii there are many great things to do. Therefore it’s important to decide on the top few adventures you want to partake in while you are there.

Diamond Head State Monument

One adventure you should participate in at least once is hiking Diamond Head State Monument. Diamond Head is considered a State Monument. It can be seen from the Waikiki beach and is a short bus ride or drive away. Diamond Head is a crater that is about a 1.5-mile hike round trip. Be aware there will be a tunnel you will have to walk through and at times steep stairs. However, the views of Oahu are amazing from the top of Diamond Head. You will want to make sure to bring

  1. Good walking shoes
  2. Water
  3. Reef-safe Sunscreen
  4. Healthy Snack for energy
  5. A hat
